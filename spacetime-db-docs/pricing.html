FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Enterprise Pricing work?
    With SpacetimeDB Enterprise we work with you to take your idea all the way through from concept to production. During development we provide direct engineering support to help build your application and charge a monthly fee. When it's time to deploy into production, we charge per node you deploy so that the cost scales with you.
  • Do databases have resource limits?
    In SpacetimeDB Maincloud databases are limited only by the physical capacity of the machines that they are scheduled to run on. Maincloud is configured with enterprise grade machines which have upwards of 80 cores and 256 GBs of memory. With SpacetimeDB's incredible performance and efficiency you can get an enormous amount of throughput from a single machine.
  • How do I scale horizontally with SpacetimeDB?
    For extreme scale where a single machine isn't sufficient, applications can also be broken up into multiple databases which communicate via inter-module communication (IMC) to allow you to horizontally scale. IMC makes it incredibly easy to transfer data between databases with a guarantee of exactly-once processing.
